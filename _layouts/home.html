Hey there. This is my story.

Due to various events in my adolescence, I grapple with Avoidant Personality Disorder and anxieties. Honestly, I'm not a fan of those labels. Through my teenage and early adult years, I've often carried a negative mindset, contemplated shortcuts, hardly savored life, and barely explored the world. But there came a moment when I rebelled. I refuse to squander my life. I want to savor it to the fullest. I wholeheartedly embarked on the path of personal improvement. I still have a million things to refine, including some negative habits I've carried since my teenage years. This is my little corner where I'll document my profound personal transformation.

I've segmented human capabilities into four categories to structure my discussions:

  • Occupational: Notes and comments pertaining to my endeavors toward a better lifestyle.
  • Body: I'll delve into my fitness journey, nutrition, routines to nurture my body...
  • Mind: Related to cultivating inner peace, exploring books, mindfulness, mental models...
  • Expressive: This is where I'll share opinions, reflections, expressions of gratitude, influences, goals...

With utmost authenticity and pure intent, devoid of any pretense. Love will forever remain the answer.
